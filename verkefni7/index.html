Kaleo

Kaleo is an Icelandic rock band known for their bluesy, soulful sound and powerful performances. Formed in 2012, the band gained international recognition with their breakout single Way Down We Go, which was featured in numerous films, TV shows, and commercials.

Their music blends rock, blues, folk, and indie influences, often drawing inspiration from their Icelandic roots. Fronted by lead singer and guitarist JJ Julius Son, Kaleo's deep, raspy vocals and atmospheric instrumentals set them apart.

Their 2016 album A/B brought them mainstream success, featuring hits like No Good and All the Pretty Girls. In 2021, they followed up with Surface Sounds, further showcasing their versatility and storytelling ability.

Known for their electrifying live performances and ability to switch from raw, gritty rock to haunting acoustic ballads, Kaleo continues to captivate audiences worldwide.
